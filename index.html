About Me

Hi! My name is Nikash Khanna and I am currently a senior at Monta Vista High School. I love problem solving and using technology to make a positive impact on the world. Programming is a big part of my life, and I have used a variety of languages, like Swift, Java, Python, C, C++, and HTML/CSS to build applications that can have a real, tangible difference.
